Who We Are

Born from the belief that no one should be left behind

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla — headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. A Section 8 registered non-profit driven by collective compassion.

We focus on underserved communities, providing food, education, skills, and compassion to individuals and families who need it most. Our work spans 28 states and grows every single day.

Every rupee raised is directed towards essential causes — food, education, women empowerment, animal welfare, and social upliftment. No overhead theatrics. Just impact.
